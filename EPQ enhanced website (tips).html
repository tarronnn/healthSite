Eat Lots Of Fruits & Veg

Rich in vitamins and minerals, aim for 5 portions of a variety of fruit and veg every day.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 6–8 glasses of water daily.

Cut down on saturated fat and sugar

On average, men should have no more than 30g of saturated fat a day. On average, women should have no more than 20g of saturated fat a day.

Do not skip breakfast

Start the day with a high fibre and low fat breakfast for steady energy.

Portion Control

Eat smaller meals more frequently and reduece the frequency of late night meals

Combine Diet & Exercise

For ideal results combine your healthy diet with regualr exercise
